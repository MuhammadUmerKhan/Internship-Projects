Diabetes Facts
Understanding the risk factors
Blood Glucose
High blood glucose (sugar) is a key indicator of diabetes. Normal fasting glucose levels are below 100 mg/dL.
BMI Impact
Higher BMI increases diabetes risk. A BMI over 25 is considered overweight and over 30 is considered obese.
Genetic Factors
Family history plays a significant role in diabetes risk. The pedigree function quantifies this genetic influence.
